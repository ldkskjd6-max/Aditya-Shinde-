Examination Form Submission

All regular Semester‑II students and backlog Semester‑I students are informed to fill the Summer 2026 Examination Form online at:

sgbau.ucanapply.com

After filling the form, take the printout and submit it to the college office with the required examination fee within the scheduled time.

Students must submit the form printout; otherwise the examination seat number will not be issued.

Some courses may require a late fee of ₹400 while submitting the form.
